About Us

Mission

khamar.ai delivers measurable ROI to South Asian farmers through AI-powered agricultural tools designed for low-connectivity, multilingual environments—starting with feed optimization and expanding to comprehensive farm intelligence.

Vision

To be the trusted intelligence partner for every farmer in South Asia, transforming traditional agriculture through accessible, affordable AI.

Farmer-First

Technology that works for farmers, not the other way around.

Evidence-Based

Grounded in scientific and data-driven practices.

Accessible

Works offline, in Bangla, and on low-cost devices.

Integrity

Transparent, ethical, and data-responsible innovation.

Our Work

Farm Intelligence
